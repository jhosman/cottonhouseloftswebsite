The Upper Cottonhouse Lofts at Appleton Mills

Resident Services

Trash and Recycling

Residents should use the dumpster at the back of the building, outside the gate at the back of the underpass, to discard their trash. Dumpster area is locked from both the inside and outside, so please remember to bring your key. There is currently no recycling facility available.

Upper Cottonhouse Lofts Yahoo Group

Residents of the sixth floor in the Cottonhouse Lofts are welcome to join the UCL Yahoo Group, where residents can send and recieve emails with other UCL residents. The UCL Yahoo Group is meant to be used only for communications relevant to the Cottonhouse Lofts, and only residents of the UCL may join.

Bicycle Room

There are storage racks for 18 bicycles in the Upper Cottonhouse Lofts, which are available to sixth floor residents only. A $25 deposit for a key to the room is required, and rental of space in the room is $25 per six-month period, per bicycle.

If you would like to use this room to store your bicycle(s), please remit $50 to Jeff Prindle at Great North Property Management, 100 Daniel Webster Highway, Nashua, NH 03060. Your check must be made payable to the "Upper Cottonhouse Lofts Condo Assoc."

With your check, please provide a description of your bicycle. Bicycles not registered with the Association will be removed and discarded.

Please contact Jeff Pridle with any questions about the bicycle room.
