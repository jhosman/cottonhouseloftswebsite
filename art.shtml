The Upper Cottonhouse Lofts at Appleton Mills

Art Collection of the Upper Cottonhouse Lofts

The art collection of the Upper Cottonhouse Lofts currently consists of 29 black-and-white photographs by Lowell artists Jessica Hosman, Sarah Coughtry, and Melissa Santarpio. The photographs depict various buildings and landscapes in Lowell, and were printed by hand in the darkroom on fiber-based, silver gelatin paper. The art collection is only available to residents of the Cottonhouse Lofts and their visitors, but please enjoy a slideshow of the images below.
