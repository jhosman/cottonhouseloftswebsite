The Upper Cottonhouse Lofts at Appleton Mills

Meetings

The board of trustees meets every two months—on the fourth Tuesday of each odd-numbered month. Any owner is welcome to attend. Please contact one of the trustees or the Property Manager for the time and location of upcoming meetings.

Annual meetings are normally held during the winter months, and all owners should plan to attend.
