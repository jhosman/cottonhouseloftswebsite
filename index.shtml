The Upper Cottonhouse Lofts at Appleton Mills

Welcome

Welcome to the website of the Upper Cottonhouse Lofts at Appleton Mills. We are located on the sixth floor of the Appleton Mills, in historic Lowell, Massachusetts. Within minutes' walking distance from the Cottonhouse Lofts are the cobblestone streets of downtown Lowell, as well as the MBTA commuter trains into Boston.
